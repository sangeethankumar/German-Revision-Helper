**Explanation:**
The prefix **ur-** strengthens meaning, roughly equivalent to *“very”* or *“original/prime.”*
Common examples:

* *uralt* → very old
* *ursprünglich* → originally
* *urplötzlich* → extremely sudden (used in narration)
